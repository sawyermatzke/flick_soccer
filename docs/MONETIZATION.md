# MONETIZATION

Status: draft v0

## Product posture
Optimize for stability, legitimacy, and non-pay-to-win monetization.

## Recommended launch model
- free download
- core gameplay available without paywall fragmentation
- monetization through cosmetics and presentation layers

## Candidate monetization items
- board skins
- ball skins / trails
- team/theme packs
- audio packs / announcer variants
- premium cosmetic bundles

## Avoid
- power advantages
- paid gameplay stats
- anything that harms competitive trust

## Why this fits the architecture
A cosmetics/theme model aligns with the requirement to support swappable branding. The content system should be able to load alternate names, logos, board art, and presentation themes without touching rules code.
